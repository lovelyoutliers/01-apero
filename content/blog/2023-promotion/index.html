Delighted to share that Dr Jen Dykxhoorn has been promoted to Principal Research Fellow (Associate Professor) at UCL within the Division of Psychiatry’s Department of Epidemiology and Applied Clinical Research.

Thanks to all of the extraordinary mentors, collaborators, colleagues, and the psychiatric epidemiology community which has been tremendously supportive over the years. Looking forward to continuing breaking new ground in epidemiology & public health research.

About UCL

UCL has been consistently ranked as one of the top universities worldwide QS World University Rankings. Dr Dykxhoorn works within the UCL Division of Psychiatry, one of the largest interdisciplinary centres of mental health research in the UK, covering epidemiology, neuroscience, palliative care, and applied clinical research.

Work with us

Dr Dykxhoorn supervises PhD students within the Division of Psychiatry. Prospective students can contact Jen with a summary their research interests and can get more information about research degrees in the Division of Psychiatry here

Study with us

Jen leads the Social Determinants of Global Health MSc module in the Institute of Epidemiology and Health Care. Spaces are open to postgraduate students at UCL, including those enrolled in the MSc in Social Epidemiology, MSc Population Health, and MSc Global Health and Development. She also lectures on several modules within the Division of Psychiatry’s Mental HEalth Sciences MSc.
